Why should you inline CSS in HTML emails?

As email clients are unpredictable, and even today many clients strip styles that are not inlined, it is important to inline your CSS before sending. Don't risk your transactional emails or marketing campaigns falling apart.

Our CSS inliner is powered by Juice. You can inline your CSS above then copy the output into your email service provider or codebase. Or you can use a Juice module and inline the CSS in your codebase. We use this for our own templates and as it is an open source project it is constantly being working on and improved.
